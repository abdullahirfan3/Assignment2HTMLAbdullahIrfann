Abdullah Irfan’s 2004 BMW 545i

Abdullah’s 2004 BMW 545i is today’s build of the day. The car combines the 4.4 L V8 with a rare SMG gearbox and a stage 2+ clutch. Only a limited number of these cars were built in this configuration, which makes the drivetrain setup stand out.

Styling upgrades include BMW M5 front and rear bumpers that give the car a more aggressive look, along with TSW Jarama three-piece wheels. A quad-tip valvetronic exhaust system lets the V8 sound come through while still being usable every day.

Updated LCI headlights and tail lights modernize the exterior. A custom ducktail spoiler and tinted windows finish the outside with a clean, silver-on-black theme.

Inside the car, Logic 7 surround sound speakers, M5 seats, and an M5 steering wheel bring the feel of a higher trim level. Solowerks S1 coilovers lower the car and improve handling at the same time. The result is a 545i that balances comfort, style, and performance in one package.
